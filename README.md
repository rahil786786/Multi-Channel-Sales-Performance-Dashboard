Project Overview
This is a high-impact, interactive Sales Performance Dashboard developed to transform raw transactional data into actionable business intelligence. It is designed to track sales across a multi-channel environment, effectively managing performance for both Online and Offline operations. This dashboard is an essential tool for managers, analysts, and stakeholders looking to understand trends, compare channel efficacy, and optimize overall business strategy.

Key Analytical Features & Components
The dashboard enables powerful drill-down analysis across multiple business dimensions:

Sales Channel Analysis: Instantly compare the performance of Online and Offline sales channels, viewing total sales figures and their percentage split to understand contribution and dominance.

Geographical Insights: Analyze sales distribution across key regions and major cities (e.g., Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai, Kolkata, Nagpur), highlighting top-performing markets and areas needing focused marketing effort.

Monthly Sales Trends: A time-series chart shows monthly sales progression for both channels, allowing for quick identification of seasonal peaks and troughs in the business cycle.

Product Performance Deep Dive:

Category View: Filter sales data by high-level categories such as Electronics, Clothing, and Home Decor.

Sub-Category View: Drill down further into specific products, tracking sales for items like Mobile, Laptop, Shirt, Jacket, Sofa, and Perfume.

Payment Mode Segmentation: Understand customer preferences by analyzing sales split across various payment methods, including COD (Cash on Delivery), Card, UPI, and Wallet.

Order & Delivery Status Tracking: Monitor key logistics metrics such as Delivered, Returned, In-Transit, and Cancelled orders, providing visibility into operational efficiency.

Utility and Impact
Strategic Channel Comparison: Easily compare product performance (e.g., Clothing) in specific cities (e.g., Mumbai) across the Online and Offline channels to allocate resources effectively.

Data-Driven Decision Making: Use the interactive slicers to isolate specific segments to uncover precise market opportunities and operational challenges.

Comprehensive Performance Monitoring: Provides a single-view snapshot of crucial KPIs, empowering leaders to make timely, informed business decisions.
